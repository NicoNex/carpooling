STRPTIME
Per convertire il tempo da una stringa ben formata in time_t usa strptime

char *strptime(const char *s, const char *format, struct tm *tm);

http://man7.org/linux/man-pages/man3/strptime.3.html


STRFTIME
Per fare il contrario usare strftime

size_t strftime(char *str, size_t maxsize, const char *format, const struct tm *timeptr);

https://www.tutorialspoint.com/c_standard_library/c_function_strftime.htm


MKTIME
use mktime to convert from 'struct tm *' to 'time_t'

time_t mktime(struct tm *timeptr);

https://pubs.opengroup.org/onlinepubs/7908799/xsh/mktime.html

usare l'epoch come id univoco per i guidatori porchiddio
